Access & Security

We issue credentials by hand — not by automation. Here is why, and exactly what to expect after you subscribe.

01

Subscribe via Stripe

Choose the tier that suits your needs — Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum. Your payment is processed securely through Stripe. Once confirmed, you are ready for the next step.

02

Contact us to activate

Send a brief email to support@glexicon.uk letting us know you have subscribed. Include your name and the email address used at checkout. That is all we need to get started.

03

We verify and prepare your environment

We confirm your subscription, assign you a dedicated deployment, and generate a database that belongs exclusively to your account. No two clients share data or credentials — ever.

04

Your credentials arrive securely

We send your unique API key and access URL directly to your email. You will typically hear from us within 24 hours on business days. From there, your dashboard is ready to use.

What this means for you

  • Your API key is unique to your account and should never be shared with anyone outside your organisation.
  • Your database is generated fresh for your deployment — completely isolated from every other client.
  • Manual onboarding means a real person reviews your account before credentials are issued. This protects you and the integrity of the protocol.
  • If you ever need to rotate your API key or have any concerns about your access, contact support@glexicon.uk and we will take care of it promptly.
  • Glexicon credentials and the underlying protocol are covered by trade secret protections. Your subscription agreement includes confidentiality terms.
