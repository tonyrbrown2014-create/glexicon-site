Access & Security

The website and Dashboard HQ you're using now are already yours — configured for your account, ready to use. The one thing still ahead of you is requesting your Protocol Engine, which we issue by hand to protect the trade secret it's built on.

01

Your environment is ready

Your tier, Tick Tock Points allocation, and node licences are already configured into the Dashboard HQ you're viewing right now. There's nothing further to activate — open your Dashboard HQ from the navigation above whenever you're ready.

02

Request your Protocol Engine

The Protocol Engine — the component that actually generates your tokens — is issued separately, and only by hand. Email support@glexicon.uk with the address you used at checkout, and we'll verify your account before releasing it.

03

Your engine arrives securely

We send your download link directly to your registered email, typically within 48 business hours. From there, the engine runs entirely on your own equipment — how it works, and how you use it, is never visible to Glexicon.

What This Means For You

  • Your Dashboard HQ is already private and pre-configured for your account — there's nothing left to activate to start using it.
  • The Protocol Engine is distributed manually and only as compiled binaries — never as source code — to protect the underlying method as a trade secret.
  • Every node runs entirely on your own equipment. Glexicon has no server, sandbox, or hosted instance handling your data at any point.
  • If you need your engine reissued, or have any access concerns, contact support@glexicon.uk and we'll take care of it directly.
  • Your Protocol Engine, dashboard, and underlying tokenisation method are protected as confidential trade secrets under your subscription agreement.
