03   The Core Challenge
What You're Already Dealing With

Three failure modes of retail catalog architecture.

  • Speed
    Slow product lookups kill conversion at the point of intent. Search latency, filter lag, and variant resolution delays push customers to competitors before the page finishes loading.
  • Scale
    Catalog growth outpaces the database architecture beneath it. Millions of SKUs, thousands of attribute fields, and continuous variant additions inflate storage costs without improving the shopper experience.
  • Sync
    Multi-channel inventory sync creates data drift and stockout risk. When the source of truth is a slow central database, every channel integration is racing against stale data — and losing.

05   Efficiency & Architecture
How the Protocol Works

Three pillars. One unified protocol.

Pillar I

Attribute Compression

Product attributes and variant tags are mapped to minimal-footprint tokens at ingest. Deprecated SKUs, ghost listings, and duplicate attribute records are pruned before they reach the store.

Pillar II

Edge-Native Resolution

Catalog lookups execute at the token layer — directly inside the commerce pipeline. No external database call. Classification and routing happen before the storefront requests the product record.

Pillar III

Memory Optimisation

Token store footprint shrinks by 90× through precision mapping and continuous optimisation tuned to retail catalog access patterns and seasonal demand cycles.
