Enterprise Licence Agreement
The hardened agreement governing institutional access to the Glexicon Monster Protocol and its sovereign deployment infrastructure.

1. Parties

This Agreement is entered into between Glexicon ("Licensor") and the subscribing institution ("Customer") as formally identified at the point of enterprise subscription. This Agreement supersedes all prior representations, negotiations, or agreements relating to the subject matter herein.

2. Definitions

TermDefinition
ProtocolThe Glexicon Monster data tokenisation framework, its logic, mathematics, binary components, and all associated software.
Deployment NodeEach licensed, hardened instance of the downloadable Protocol package — comprising the Protocol software, Dashboard interface, and unique licence credential — activated and operated by Customer constituting a single licensed point of Protocol execution.
Deployment ScopeThe total number of authorised Deployment Nodes and approved site locations as specified in the Customer's subscription tier and enterprise contract.

Read-Only Archive StateThe post-term operational mode under which previously indexed Tokens remain accessible for retrieval but no new tokenisation activity may be initiated.

3. Grant of Licence & Deployment Scope

Glexicon grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence strictly bounded by the Customer's approved Deployment Scope. This licence authorises Customer to:

—

Deploy and operate the Protocol across the number of Deployment Nodes specified in Customer's active tier

—

Execute Protocol operations solely at the approved site locations identified in Customer's enterprise contract

—

Generate, store, and query Tokens for Customer's internal institutional data

—

Operate in Read-Only Archive State upon licence expiry, subject to Section 5

Customer may not:

—

Deploy the Protocol beyond the licensed number of Deployment Nodes or approved site locations

—

Reverse engineer, decompile, clone, or disassemble any Deployment Node or Protocol component

—

Duplicate, redistribute, or sublicense any Deployment Node package, licence credential, or activation key beyond the licensed Deployment Scope

—

Transfer, sublicence, or share Protocol access with any third party outside Customer's institution

—

Disclose the method, mathematics, architecture, or inner workings of the Protocol to any party

4. Term, Expiry & Read-Only Archive State

This licence is granted for an initial annual term commencing on the date of confirmed subscription. Upon expiry:

—

Active Protocol access — new tokenisation, Dashboard updates, and support — ceases immediately upon non-renewal

—

Read-Only Archive State — previously indexed Tokens remain locally accessible on Customer's Deployment Nodes for a period of 90 days post-expiry, solely for data retrieval purposes

—

After 90 days — Protocol access is fully deactivated across all Deployment Nodes. Customer retains their own data but loses all Protocol-driven retrieval and indexing capability

Renewal: Automatic annual renewal unless either party provides 60 days' written notice prior to term end. Renewal pricing is confirmed in writing no less than 90 days before renewal date.

6. Deployment, Activation & Customer Responsibility

Upon confirmed subscription, Glexicon shall provide Customer with secure access to download the Deployment Node package — comprising the Protocol software, Dashboard interfaces, installation documentation, and unique licence activation credentials — for each Deployment Node within Customer's licensed Deployment Scope. Access is granted immediately upon payment confirmation via Customer's secure client portal.

Initial Custody & Deployment Autonomy The Customer acquires full custody of the Protocol and website architecture package immediately upon download. Deployment and node cloning may be executed entirely at the Customer's convenience and operational timeline. Project acceptance is deemed absolute upon the initial download of the asset package. From the point of download,Customer assumes full responsibility for:

—

Infrastructure Security: Each Deployment Node must be installed and operated within infrastructure appropriate to Customer's institutional security classification, including access-controlled environments and standard cybersecurity safeguards

—

Operational Integrity: Deployment Nodes must be operated within the technical specifications published in the accompanying installation documentation, including supported environments and resource allocation

—

Credential Custody: Customer is responsible for safeguarding all licence activation credentials. Compromised credentials must be reported immediately so Glexicon can reissue and revoke access

—

Tamper Integrity: Any attempt to circumvent, modify, or bypass licence activation controls on a Deployment Node constitutes a material breach of this Agreement and may result in immediate licence suspension

7. Token Allocation (Tick Tock Points)

Customer receives an annual allocation of Tick Tock Points as defined by their licence tier. Points may be applied to:

—

Discounts on annual licence renewal

—

Dedicated support and engineering packages

—

Institutional training and onboarding programmes

—

Approved transfer to institutional affiliates with Glexicon's prior written consent

Points carry no cash value and are not redeemable for currency. Unused Points expire at the end of each annual term and do not roll over unless otherwise agreed in writing.

8. Deployment Audit Rights

To protect the integrity of the Protocol and ensure compliance with the licensed Deployment Scope, Glexicon reserves the right to conduct periodic Deployment Audits.

—

Frequency: No more than once per calendar year, unless a specific compliance concern has been identified

—

Notice: Glexicon will provide a minimum of 14 days' written notice prior to any audit

—

Scope: Audits are limited to verifying the number of active Deployment Nodes, their registered deployment locations, and Dashboard access logs — no Customer data is reviewed or extracted

—

Findings: If an audit reveals deployment beyond the licensed scope, Customer agrees to immediately upgrade to the applicable tier and reimburse Glexicon for any underpaid fees for the period of excess deployment

13. Termination & Offboarding

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing 60 days' written notice prior to the annual renewal date. In the event of material breach by Customer, Glexicon may terminate immediately upon written notice.

Upon termination, the following offboarding procedure applies:

—

Deployment Package: Customer retains the locally installed Deployment Node software for archival reference, though all Protocol execution capability is deactivated per the Read-Only Archive terms below

—

Protocol Access: Remote Dashboard access, support services, and Protocol updates are deactivated on the termination date

—

Read-Only Archive: Customer's existing Token index remains locally accessible on installed Deployment Nodes for 90 days post-termination for data retrieval only

—

After 90 Days: Protocol execution capability is fully deactivated. Customer's own data remains intact and accessible through Customer's own systems

—

Confidentiality Survival: Obligations under Section 9 (Confidentiality) survive termination indefinitely
