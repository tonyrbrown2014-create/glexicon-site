4. Term, Expiry & Read-Only Archive State

This subscription license is granted for an initial annual term commencing on the date of confirmed subscription. Upon expiry:

—

Active Protocol Access: The legal right to execute new tokenization processes, deploy new satellite leaf nodes, or receive technical support ceases automatically upon non-renewal.

—

Read-Only Archive State: Previously processed tokens remain accessible within the Customer's controlled environment strictly for local data retrieval and historical verification purposes under a passive, non-active operational state.

—

Contractual Cessation & Legal Boundaries: Glexicon maintains zero network access, remote commands, or backdoor mechanisms to alter, terminate, or manipulate the Customer's isolated environment. The Customer retains full ownership of their underlying data files and generated tokens. However, upon subscription expiry, the legal license to execute or utilize the Protocol Engine for any active processes completely terminates. Any continued unauthorized execution of the Protocol Engine software past the active subscription term constitutes a material breach of this Agreement and software copyright infringement.

Renewal: This agreement automatically renews for consecutive annual terms unless either party provides sixty (60) days' written notice of non-renewal prior to the end of the current term. Renewal pricing will be confirmed in writing no less than ninety (90) days before the renewal date.
