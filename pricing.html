Licensing Tiers

Annual enterprise licensing with Tick Tock Points accrued for each tier. All tiers include access to the core Glexicon protocol.

Protection Against Massive Redemption

Structural safeguards ensure points remain a loyalty mechanism rather than a financial liability. Each rule is designed to protect platform integrity while sustaining long-term customer engagement.

Expiration

Points expire 90 days after license termination.

Annual Cap

Maximum 20% of the renewal fee can be offset with points per year.

Blackout Periods

No redemptions are permitted during the first license year.

Notice Period

60 days advance written notice required before any redemption event.

Minimum Holding

Customer must maintain an active license to redeem any accrued points.

Non-Transferable

Points cannot be sold, traded, or transferred to any third party.

Redemption Rules

A concise reference for the conditions governing all point redemption activity across all tiers.

Max Redemption Per Renewal

20% of the annual license fee.

Expiry Window

90 days after license termination.

Redemption Notice

60 days written notice required in all cases.

First-Year Restriction

No redemption permitted in year one of any license agreement.

Transferability

Not allowed. Points are non-transferable under all circumstances.

Cash Value

None. Points are not currency and carry no monetary equivalent.
